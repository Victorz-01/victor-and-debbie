The Day We First Met

It wasn't just any day; it was during a special monthly program at church. Amidst the crowd and the shared faith of Deeper Life, our paths finally crossed. What started as a spiritual gathering turned into the most important introduction of my life. In that sacred space, I didn't just find a friend, I found the person I wanted to walk through life with.

Getting to Know Each Other

Our early days were built on the simple, sweet joy of 2go chats and those precious, few phone calls we never wanted to end. Because we lived for those moments, every special monthly program at church became even more significant, it was our chance to finally see each other in person. It was in those long nights of typing back and forth, paired with the quiet moments we shared at Deeper Life, that I truly discovered Debbie's heart. I realised she was the one because, whether through a screen or across a church hall, her kindness and faith shone brighter than anything else

We Made It Official

I decided to go old-school and put my heart on paper. I poured everything into a letter and trusted my friend, Jerry, to act as my messenger and get it to her. I'd tucked my phone number inside, just hoping she'd use it. I still remember the feeling when my phone finally lit up with her reply. It was such a simple 'yes,' but it changed everything. Just like that, we were official, and I haven't stopped smiling since.

First Anniversary

Our first year together was low-key, but it meant the world to me. I was away at boarding school at the time, so we couldn't be together in person, but we didn't let the distance get in the way. We spent the whole day tucked away in our own little world, just chatting and reflecting on everything we'd learned about each other since that first letter. It was a simple day, but it showed me just how strong 'us' really was.

Growing Stronger Together

This was the season where us became a rock. Navigating university life together at OAU made every day feel like an adventure. We were constantly by each other's side, becoming favorite study partners and spending long hours reading together in AUD and Philosophy before rewarding ourselves with simple dates going out to eat. It wasn't always easy, though. Moving from 2go chats to the reality of being in the same space every day brought its own challenges. We had our fair share of misunderstandings, and there were even moments when things got so heavy we thought about breaking up. But honestly? Those low points were where we grew the most. Learning how to fight for each other, resolve our differences, and choose to stay is what made our bond unbreakable. We went from those early digital whispers to building a life that is beautiful, messy, and completely ours
